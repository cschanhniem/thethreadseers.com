# Stretch 2: Academy Arrival (Discovery Arc)

## Purpose
Move Lyra into Threadweaver Academy, form the quartet under stress, and establish the first clear line of harm: control without consent.

## Chapters
- **Chapter 5: The Severance (`5.md`)**: Iris Bookstore meeting; Ben collapses; Harlow intervenes; the diagnosis lands.
- **Chapter 6: The Weight of Memory (`6.md`)**: Harlow POV; his rationale and pressure; surveillance begins.
- **Chapter 7: The Father's Shadow (`7.md`)**: Blackwood starts collecting what the official record will not keep; Harlow escalates his watch.
- **Chapter 8: The Pressure Cooker (`8.md`)**: Intake stress spikes into a first campus incident; the quartet forms through action.
- **Chapter 9: The Silver Burn (`9.md`)**: Travel to the Academy; the "M.B." letter; Harlow's lecture tightens the noose.
- **Chapter 10: The Gauntlet (`10.md`)**: Team trial; competence bonding; institutional rules show teeth.
- **Chapter 11: The Magnus Conduit (`11.md`)**: The Maze and the blueprint; Animus Argenti stops being hypothetical.
